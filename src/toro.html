Toro Arena

OpenGL-Based 3D Scrolling Game

Gameplay

Toro Arena is a class project which integrates gaming and technologies.
The player controls the bull.
The goal is to get the bull to the end of the road unscratched.
The bull could move to left or right to avoid the obstacles.

Technologies

We used toon shading to give it an anime style look.
We used L-systems to generate random plants along the road.
We used depth of field at the beginning of the game to show a preview of the map.
We used particle effects to simulate dust when the bull moves.
Other technologies: lighting, keyboard control, collision detection.
